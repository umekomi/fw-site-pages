What is FriendWorld?

FriendWorld is an internet fiction project. It's about an axolotl and a dog-rabbit-creature who go on fun, magical adventures. The first part (Book 0) is complete, and contains about 23k words, or 80 paperback pages. If you enjoyed Homestuck, Worm, or 17776, consider giving FriendWorld a try!

This site also serves as a repository for all projects made under the Umekomi alias. This will include music, and possibly artwork and blog posts.

Everything is subject to revision, including parts already published.

FriendWorld is not made for children.
